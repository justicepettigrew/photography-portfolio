JUSTICE ANNE PETTIGREW

I am a curious, self-taught creative based in Boston. I work as a graphic designer at a start-up solving the student debt crisis, and my creative passions spill into photography and writing as well. I have a deep desire to learn and create, and you can find that embodied on this website. I grew up in Hong Kong, and have added Chicago and Boston to the spaces that encapsulate home for me. I am a lover of sunshine, skateboards, mom jeans, dad caps, reading, podcasts, my cat, and all things creative.
